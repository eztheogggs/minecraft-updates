Welcome to the Future of Minecraft

Mojang has shifted from major updates like 1.22 to frequent "game drops." The next drop, post-Spring to Life (1.21.5), introduces new mobs and mechanics. Snapshots 25w03a to 25w18a (Jan-Apr 2025) reveal what's next. Explore now!

New Features in the Next Game Drop

New Cow Variants

Cold cows in snowy biomes; warm cows in deserts and badlands.

Happy Ghasts & Ghastlings

Raise ghastlings from dried ghasts and ride happy ghasts!

Locator Bar

Find friends in multiplayer with a toggleable UI.

Firefly Bush

Light level 2 bushes for ambient build lighting.

Updated Lead Mechanics

Leash mobs to boats or create caravans.

Harness Crafting

Craft harnesses for happy ghasts with wool colors.
